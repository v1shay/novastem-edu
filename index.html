How It Works

1. We Partner With Teachers

We coordinate directly with educators to fit workshops seamlessly into a single class period.

2. We Bring Everything

All materials, equipment, and setup are handled by NovaSTEM at no cost to the school.

3. Students Run Real Experiments

Students actively build, test, and observe across STEM disciplines.

4. Learning That Sticks

Hands-on work builds curiosity, confidence, and deeper understanding.
