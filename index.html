NovaSTEM

Hands-On Science. Real Curiosity. Early Impact.

NovaSTEM is a student-led nonprofit dedicated to making science tangible, exciting, and accessible for younger students. Through hands-on experiments and interactive demonstrations, NovaSTEM brings physics, chemistry, and biology out of textbooks and into the classroom.
