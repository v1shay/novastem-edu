Why It Matters

Many classrooms lack the materials and time needed for hands-on science. NovaSTEM removes those barriers by delivering free, experiment-based learning that keeps students engaged and asking questions.

Teachers report stronger participation, higher curiosity, and more confidence in discussing scientific ideas.

About the Founder

NovaSTEM was founded by Vishay Agarwal, a student passionate about making high-quality STEM education accessible early. With a background in computer science, research, and outreach, he started NovaSTEM to bring real experiments into real classrooms — especially where resources are limited.
