How It Works

1. We Partner With Teachers

We coordinate directly with educators to schedule workshops that fit seamlessly into class time.

2. We Bring Everything

NovaSTEM provides all materials, setup, and cleanup— no prep required from teachers.

3. Students Run the Experiment

Students take the lead—building, testing, and observing real STEM experiments from start to finish.

4. Learning That Sticks

Hands-on problem-solving builds curiosity, confidence, and a lasting interest in science and engineering.

What We Teach

Biology & Ecosystems

Students explore living systems through observation, data collection, and guided scientific inquiry.

Chemistry & Matter

Students investigate properties of matter, evidence-based reasoning, and experimental design through safe, hands-on activities.

Physics & Engineering

Through engineering challenges like balloon rockets, students learn about force, motion, and cause-and-effect.

NovaSTEM was founded by Vishay Agarwal, a student educator passionate about bringing rigorous, hands-on STEM experiences into elementary classrooms through real experiments.
